Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) applies to Earnr Holdings (ACN 637 125 421) and related companies, our affiliates, our product partners who issue products arranged or promoted by Earnr, and their related companies; collectively ("we", "us", "our"). Some of our product partners and affiliates may have separate, but consistent policies.

We are committed adhering to the Australian Privacy Principles contained in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) (“Privacy Act”). We recognise the importance of your privacy, and that you have a right to control how your personal information is collected and used. Our Policy outlines how we handle your information, and how you can contact us in relation to any personal information we hold about you.

      • Contact details (eg. your name, address, email, phone number);
      • information required for you to open an account with us, receive services from or do business with us. This information may include:
        • Names, occupation, contact and address details
        • Information in identification documents
        • Information about shareholders, beneficiaries and beneficial owners
        • Tax file numbers and other government-issued identification numbers
        • Date of birth and gender
        • Details about your spouse, children and other family members
        • Bank account details including bank account details that you link to your account
        • Details about your bank account balances and spending activities, account interest and financial history;
      • personal information that allows us to tailor the services we make available to you;
      • information required as part of a recruitment process;
      • information regarding the use of the Earnr websites or apps, including website users' IP addresses and the dates and times of visits.
    • We usually collect personal information when you:
      • Submit information to us (including via the Earnr website or app), eg. when you express interest in a product or service, register an account, retrieve log-in information, or request a new password;
      • request a product arranged or issued by Earnr;
      • submit information through the Earnr website;
      • register for email subscriptions, or publications through the Earnr website or app;
      • receive services from us. This may include us accessing information held by your financial institution or other third parties that we have relationships with;
      • use earnr websites or Apps;
      • deal with us (through the website, app, by email, phone or in person) or request that we contact you;
      • participate in referral arrangements, promotions or competitions run by Earnr or with the support of Earnr.
    • Sometimes it may be necessary for us to collect your personal information from a third party. For example, we may collect your personal information from a publicly available record or from your personal representative. Or you may choose to provide us with access to certain personal information stored by third parties such as social media sites. The information we may receive varies by site and is controlled by that site. By associating an account managed by a third party with your Earnr account and authorising Earnr to have access to this information, you agree that Earnr may collect, store and use this information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.
